FocusSuite. A collection of apps that help reduce digital bad habits.

FocusSuite was created with a simple idea: digital wellbeing tools do not have to be aggressive to be effective.Many modern digital wellbeing apps rely on strict blocking, forced restrictions, and intrusive reminders. While these methods can help some people, they can also feel frustrating and reduce user trust.FocusSuite takes a different approach. Instead of aggressively controlling your device, it provides a collection of lightweight tools designed to help you stay focused, reduce distractions, and build healthier digital habits naturally.

The suite currently includes four applications:

🦆 Focus Engine Pro — A Pomodoro timer designed to help you maintain focus through structured work and break sessions.
⚓ Focus Anchor — An app that encourages intentional work by allowing you to "anchor" yourself to a task and release the anchor when your work is complete.
🧭 UmbraFocus — A focus companion designed to help you stay engaged with your goals and reduce distractions.
🧮 Sonic Calc — A calculator with a unique atmosphere, combining productivity with soothing music and a pleasant user experience.

Why choose FocusSuite?

✓ Completely free
✓ No advertisements

✓ Lightweight and efficient
✓ Privacy-friendly

FocusSuite is hosted on GitHub and developed with transparency in mind. The goal is simple: provide useful digital wellbeing tools that respect your privacy, your data, and your ability to choose how you work.
